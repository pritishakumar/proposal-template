The main object is to create industry appropriate branding and to modernize the company's online presence.



How Will We Get There?


Our goal is to establish fresh new branding that would communicate the company as being well established and ‘authentic’.

We will also create a new website design that is updated in content, in addition to being visually consistent with the new branding. This new website will enable easy scalability for adding any future content.



Our Process


Phase 1: Branding

An initial interview will be conducted to provide us with a clear picture of who you are and what you stand for. This interview will provide us with a clear message of what visual identity your company needs in terms of:

  • Logo Design
  • Brand Design
  • Typography

From there, three new general brand concepts will be created and presented via slideshow. We will discuss these options and decide which direction to take, while accounting for any necessary adjustments.


Phase 2 : Website Design

With your new branding in hand, we will then go through a slideshow of more specific website designs which are appropriate for your industry, to narrow down the look and feel of your new website.

Based on that choice, two separate website designs with different layouts will be made for you to choose from. With an option chosen, we can then start designing your website’s content.


Phase 3 : Final design prototype

We will go through each page of your current website and find out what content is in need of reorganizing or refreshing. This is the time curate any new content that you would like to integrate into the website, so that we can create a layout that would best highlight your new content.

When all of the content is finalized, a page-by-page design of the website will be designed and shown to you. With the final design approved, your website is ready for development.


Phase 4: Development

Your company's Wordpress editing permissions will be shared with us, so we can access the domain and the necessary software tools. We will build the website on Wordpress, parallel to the existing company website, without affecting its operations. The website will be developed in full, in accordance to the final design prototype created during the previous phase.


Phase 5: Testing and Debugging

Upon completion of the website development, the website will be turned over to you for feedback, confirming compatibility and checking for any website bugs or glitches. All of the details and operations of the website will be checked for proper functionality. Functionality will be checked with screen readers for comfortable website accessibility, as well for compatibility with different web browsers, tablets and mobile operating systems.


Phase 6: Deployment

The website, being fully confirmed and approved by all parties, will be deployed to replace the existing operating company website. Functionality will again be confirmed in all components of the website after deployment.


Phase 7: Maintenance and Hand Off

The new functioning website will be handed off to you, along with any details and instructions needed for you to maintain and update the website yourself. Our access permissions to the website will be deactivated and full privacy for editing the website restored to you.


Phase 8: Wordpress IT Troubleshooting

This will occur throughout the other phases, as we troubleshoot issues with the Wordpress email client, and compatibility issues with company cellphones.
